1 Player: click the right song. You have 10 seconds per track to answer, you will be asked first for the name of the track and secondly for the singer/group name. You get 5 points for every correct answer and lose 5 for every incorrect one.

2 Players: Player 1 will be able to select the right answer with a mouse/trackpad. Player 2 will use the arrows keys on the keyboard, arrow left: Answer 1, up 2, down 3, right 4. The player who answer correctly first gets the 5 points. If the guess is wrong lose the 5 points and the other player can still answer and get the points. The player with more points wins.
